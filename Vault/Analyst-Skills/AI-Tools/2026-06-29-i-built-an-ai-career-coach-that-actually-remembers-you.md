## What’s new and why it matters
Most AI-powered career assistants are impressive during the first conversation. They analyze your resume, answer interview questions, and provide career advice. But there's one major problem. They forget everything about you the next time you return. They don't remember your resume improvements, previous interview performance, weak technical topics, or career goals. Every interaction starts from scratch, making the experience feel more like chatting with a search engine than working with a mentor. I wanted to solve this problem.
