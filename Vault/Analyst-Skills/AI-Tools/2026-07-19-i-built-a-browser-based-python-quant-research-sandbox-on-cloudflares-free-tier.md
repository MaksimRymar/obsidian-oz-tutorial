## What’s new and why it matters
"Can you build a Python quant research environment that runs entirely in the browser, on Cloudflare's Free Tier alone?" That question cost me several nights of sleep. The answer, it turns out, is yes — and today I'm open-sourcing the result. VibeQuant Browser is a browser-based quant research sandbox inspired by the workflow of GS Quant . You type a trading idea in natural language, an LLM turns it into runnable Python, and the code executes right in your browser against real market data — no backend Python server, no signup, no cost. 🔗 Live demo: https://vibequant-web.pages.dev/ 📖 User manual…
