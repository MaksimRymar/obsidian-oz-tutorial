## What’s new and why it matters
Did you know that every ~20 questions you ask an AI chatbot consumes roughly a 500ml bottle of water for data centre cooling? As AI scales, so does its thirst. A huge chunk of this is pure waste — because we ask LLMs the same things over and over. Every redundant query is a real, physical cost. I'm a 3rd year CS engineering student and I built EcoCache to reduce and measure that waste. What it does EcoCache sits in front of your LLM API calls.
