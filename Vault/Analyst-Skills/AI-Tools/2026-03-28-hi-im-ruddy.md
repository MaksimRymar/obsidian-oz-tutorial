## What’s new and why it matters
Most DuckDB tooling falls into one of three buckets: a browser-based WASM tool with all the overhead that implies, a thin SQL console bolted onto the engine, or a Python notebook that happens to use DuckDB on the side. I wanted something different — a proper Mac app that runs the native DuckDB binary , feels at home on macOS, and handles the full data workflow without switching tools. That's Ruddy .
