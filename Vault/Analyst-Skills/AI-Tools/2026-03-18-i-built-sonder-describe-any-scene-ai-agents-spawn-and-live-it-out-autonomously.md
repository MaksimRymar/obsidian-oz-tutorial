## What’s new and why it matters
I wanted to watch AI agents interact without directing them. Not chatbots answering questions — actual people, with histories and tensions, living inside a scene I described. So I built Sonder. What it does You type a scene. Something like: "Five coworkers watching football at someone's flat. The team is losing. One of them hasn't been paid in three weeks." Sonder generates five agents. Each gets a name, a personality, a backstory, a reason to be in that room, and something eating at them. Then it starts the room. You don't say anything. They just... go.
