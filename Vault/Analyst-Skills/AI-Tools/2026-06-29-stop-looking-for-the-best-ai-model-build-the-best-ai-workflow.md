## What’s new and why it matters
Every week, another AI model makes headlines. ChatGPT. Claude. Gemini. Perplexity. Open-source models. People spend hours comparing benchmarks. But here's the reality: Most productivity gains don't come from switching models. They come from building better workflows. Why workflows matter Each AI has different strengths. Instead of replacing one another, they complement each other. Example workflow: Research → Perplexity Brainstorm → ChatGPT Long-form writing → Claude Workspace integration → Gemini Final review → Human No single AI needs to do everything.
