## What’s new and why it matters
Every time someone in Australia wants to build a pool, knock down a house, or develop a block of townhouses, they submit a Development Application (DA) to their local council. That's public data -- 18,000+ applications across 330+ councils, updated daily. Most people don't realise how powerful this data is when you combine it with other sources.
