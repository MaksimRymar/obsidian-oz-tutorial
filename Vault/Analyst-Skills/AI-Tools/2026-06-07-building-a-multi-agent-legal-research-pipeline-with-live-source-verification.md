## What’s new and why it matters
Law firms don't need another chatbot—they need verified citations . Architecture Six agents: Research → Precedent → Analysis → Citation → Brief Writer → Filing. Each run queries public legal APIs/pages (Cornell LII, Oyez, CourtListener, GovInfo). ** Demo** Public Carpenter v. United States walkthrough: https://owl-ai-agency.com/legal-research-demo.html?autostart=1 ** Code** FastAPI orchestrator on Render; agents produce memos, briefs, TOA. Compare Why not ChatGPT alone: https://owl-ai-agency.com/compare/owl-vs-chatgpt-legal-research.html — Hobie Cunningham, OWL AI Agency
