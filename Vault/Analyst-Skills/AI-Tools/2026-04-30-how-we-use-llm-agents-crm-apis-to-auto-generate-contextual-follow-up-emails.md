## What’s new and why it matters
The follow-up email problem is not a writing problem Everyone frames the sales follow-up email as a writing quality problem. Better copy, better subject lines, better personalization. A/B test the CTA. Shorten the paragraphs. That is the wrong frame. The actual problem is context. A rep finishing their sixth call of the day does not write a bad follow-up because they lack writing skill.
