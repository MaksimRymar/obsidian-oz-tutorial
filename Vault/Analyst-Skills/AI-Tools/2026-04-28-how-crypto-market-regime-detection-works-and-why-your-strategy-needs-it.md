## What’s new and why it matters
Every crypto market exists in one of three states: trending up, trending down, or going nowhere. The difference between a profitable strategy and a losing one often comes down to knowing which state you're in right now . Crypto market regime detection is the process of classifying the current market environment into discrete states — bull, bear, or chop — using multiple data sources rather than a single indicator.
