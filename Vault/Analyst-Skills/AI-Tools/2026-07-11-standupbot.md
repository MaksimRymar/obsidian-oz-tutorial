## What’s new and why it matters
A CLI tool that reads your recent git commits and generates a standup update — Yesterday / Today / Blockers, ready to paste into Slack, Jira, or wherever your team posts standups. standup That's the command. It reads commits from the last 24 hours (configurable), classifies them, and generates the summary. Two backends, both free Provider Cost Where it runs Ollama Free 100% local Groq Free tier Cloud No subscription. Run it with Ollama and nothing leaves your machine. If you use Groq, commit messages get scanned and redacted before they're sent — private IPs, internal hostnames, GitHub tokens,…
