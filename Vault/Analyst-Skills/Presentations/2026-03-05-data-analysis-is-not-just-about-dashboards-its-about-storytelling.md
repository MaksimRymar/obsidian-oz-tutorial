## What’s new and why it matters
📊 When people hear data analysis, they often imagine dashboards, spreadsheets, and charts. But the real power lies in the story behind the numbers. Numbers alone rarely create impact. A chart may show sales increased by 20% or traffic spiked, but it doesn’t answer why these changes occurred. Why Storytelling Matters Data storytelling transforms raw numbers into a clear narrative, adding context, explanation, and purpose.
