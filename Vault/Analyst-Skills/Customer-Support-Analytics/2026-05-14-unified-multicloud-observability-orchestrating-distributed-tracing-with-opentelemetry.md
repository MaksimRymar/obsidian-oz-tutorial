## What’s new and why it matters
Distributed architectures spanning Amazon Web Services and Microsoft Azure frequently suffer from observability fragmentation. Engineering teams find themselves navigating isolated dashboards in CloudWatch and Azure Monitor, attempting to manually correlate timestamps and request IDs while the Mean Time To Resolution (MTTR) escalates.
