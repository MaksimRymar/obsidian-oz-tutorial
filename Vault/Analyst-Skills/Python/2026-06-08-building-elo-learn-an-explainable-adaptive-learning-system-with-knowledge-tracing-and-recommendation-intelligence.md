## What’s new and why it matters
Traditional online learning platforms often treat every student the same way. Two students may receive identical lessons, exercises, and recommendations despite having completely different strengths, weaknesses, and learning histories. I wanted to explore how machine learning and educational analytics could be combined to create a more personalized learning experience.
