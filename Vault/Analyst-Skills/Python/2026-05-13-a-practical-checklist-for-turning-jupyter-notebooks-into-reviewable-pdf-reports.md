## What’s new and why it matters
Jupyter notebooks are great while the work is still changing. They are less forgiving when the output has to become a PDF that someone else can review, archive, or attach to a report. Most export problems are not caused by one broken command. They usually come from small mismatches between notebook state, output size, browser rendering, LaTeX dependencies, image paths, and what the reviewer expects to read.
