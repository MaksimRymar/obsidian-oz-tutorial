## What’s new and why it matters
Last month a founder I advise told me their "ad ROI was 300%." I had to stop and ask: do you mean ROAS, or actual profit-based ROI? At a 30% gross margin those two numbers tell opposite stories — one looks healthy, the other is a loss. The formulas look so similar that they get swapped silently in the same meeting, and budget decisions quietly go backwards. TL;DR ROAS = ad revenue ÷ ad spend × 100. ROI = profit ÷ total investment × 100.
