## What’s new and why it matters
Pulled my hair out for an hour trying to connect to a random MTProto proxy I found in some Telegram channel. Wouldn't work. Tried another. Dead too. Third one? You guessed it. So I automated the whole thing. This scraper runs every hour, checks public channels for proxy links, then actually tests each one with proper fake-TLS before adding to a live list. The web page updates automatically with only working proxies, and there's a raw .txt for scripting. No more guessing. No more testing dead links by hand. Just grab the latest from https://github.com/Yagami200/free-mtproto-proxies and move on…
