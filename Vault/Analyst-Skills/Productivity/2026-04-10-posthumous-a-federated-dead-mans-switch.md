## What’s new and why it matters
Some things should only happen after you can't do them yourself. Posthumous is a self-hosted dead man's switch. You check in periodically (via phone, browser, CLI, or API call) and if you stop, it progresses through escalating stages before triggering automated actions: sending notifications, running scripts, whatever you've configured. I built it because the existing options are either cloud-hosted (you're trusting someone else's uptime for your most important automation) or single-node (one server failure and silence is indistinguishable from death).
