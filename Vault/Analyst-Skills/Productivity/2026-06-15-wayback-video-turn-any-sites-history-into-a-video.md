## What’s new and why it matters
Side project outside my usual PHP world, but worth sharing. I have a habit of Googling old websites just to see what they looked like. GitHub circa 2008. Wikipedia in 2003. The Wayback Machine has it all, but clicking through snapshots one at a time never gives you the full picture of how a site evolved. So I built wayback-video - a Python CLI tool that takes a URL, pulls its entire Wayback Machine archive, renders each snapshot with a headless browser, and assembles everything into an MP4. wayback-video https://github.com --scroll --interval year --from 2008 That's it.
