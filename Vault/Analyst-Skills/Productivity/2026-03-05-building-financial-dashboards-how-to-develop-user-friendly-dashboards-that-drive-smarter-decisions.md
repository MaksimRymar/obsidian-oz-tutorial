## What’s new and why it matters
What if the numbers aren’t the problem… but how you see them is? A startup founder once told me: “We’re making revenue. We’re raising funding. We’re tracking expenses. But I still feel blind.” Every week, they reviewed spreadsheets. Rows and rows of data. Revenue columns. Expense breakdowns. Cash balances. Everything was there. But nothing was clear. Then they built a simple financial dashboard. One screen. Five key metrics. Clear trends. And suddenly, decision-making became faster, calmer, and smarter. That’s the power of a well-designed financial dashboard. Not more data. Better visibility.…
