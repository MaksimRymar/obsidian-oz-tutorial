## What’s new and why it matters
Most developers waste months building ideas nobody asked for. They write code first, then look for validation later, then wonder why the launch dies on impact. That approach feels creative, but it is mostly expensive guessing. A better play is to start from a product that already works, then find the market it still ignores. That is the whole logic behind App Store geo-arbitrage .
