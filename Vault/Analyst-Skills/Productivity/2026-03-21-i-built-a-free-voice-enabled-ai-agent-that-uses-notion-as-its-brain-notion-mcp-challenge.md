## What’s new and why it matters
What I Built NotionMind — a free, voice-enabled AI agent that treats your Notion workspace as its long-term memory. You speak or type a note. It auto-titles it, tags it, and saves it to Notion. You ask a question. It searches your Notion and answers out loud. You add a research task. It searches the web, writes the results back to Notion, and marks the task done — all while you sleep. Zero cost. No subscriptions. No data leaving your machine except to Notion and Groq's free tier.
