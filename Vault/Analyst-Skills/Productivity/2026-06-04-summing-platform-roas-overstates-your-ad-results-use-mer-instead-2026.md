## What’s new and why it matters
Google Ads reports ROAS 400%. Meta reports 350%. On each dashboard, both look great. But add the two numbers together to judge "overall ad efficiency" and you're looking at more revenue than actually exists — because each platform counts the same purchase as its own win. That gap quietly inflates how well you think your advertising is doing.
