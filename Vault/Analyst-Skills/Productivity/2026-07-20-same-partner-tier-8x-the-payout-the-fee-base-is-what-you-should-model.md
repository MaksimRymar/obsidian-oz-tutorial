## What’s new and why it matters
Two operators can sit on the exact same partner tier at an exchange and collect payouts that differ by 8x. The percentage is identical. The base it multiplies is not. This trips up a lot of people who evaluate a partner program by reading the headline number and stopping there. The number is a share of fees actually paid by the traders attributed to you — not a share of their volume, and not a function of how many of them there are.
