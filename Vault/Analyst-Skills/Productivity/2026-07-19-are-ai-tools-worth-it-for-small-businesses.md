## What’s new and why it matters
Workflow fit before feature count Buying an AI tool is easy. Proving that it improves a real workflow is the difficult part. Start with one recurring task, define the result you need, and test the tool against the current process. Then verify plan limits, integrations, support, cancellation terms, and the controls needed to review its output. A practical evaluation path Choose one repeatable task with a measurable outcome.
