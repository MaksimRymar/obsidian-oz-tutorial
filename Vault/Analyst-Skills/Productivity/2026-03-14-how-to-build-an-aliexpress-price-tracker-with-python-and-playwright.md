## What’s new and why it matters
AliExpress is a goldmine for competitive pricing, but those prices are notoriously volatile. For dropshippers or market analysts, manual price checking is a losing game. Automating this process is the only way to stay competitive. By building a custom price monitor, you can track competitor SKUs daily, identify genuine discounts versus "fake" sales, and build a historical dataset for better purchasing decisions.
