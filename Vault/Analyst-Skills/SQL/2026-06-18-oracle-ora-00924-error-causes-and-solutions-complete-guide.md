## What’s new and why it matters
ORA-00924: Missing BY Keyword in Oracle SQL ORA-00924 is a syntax error thrown by the Oracle parser when it expects the keyword BY as part of a compound keyword clause — such as GROUP BY , ORDER BY , or PARTITION BY — but cannot find it. This error is caught at the parsing stage before any data is accessed, meaning your data is safe, but the query simply will not execute until the syntax is corrected.
