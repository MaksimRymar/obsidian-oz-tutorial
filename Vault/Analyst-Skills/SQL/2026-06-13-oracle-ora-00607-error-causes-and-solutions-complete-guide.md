## What’s new and why it matters
ORA-00607: Internal Error Occurred While Making a Change to a Data Block ORA-00607 is an Oracle internal error that fires when the database engine encounters an unexpected condition while attempting to modify a data block. It almost always appears alongside ORA-00600 (the generic internal error), and the argument list of ORA-00600 is your primary clue for root cause analysis.
