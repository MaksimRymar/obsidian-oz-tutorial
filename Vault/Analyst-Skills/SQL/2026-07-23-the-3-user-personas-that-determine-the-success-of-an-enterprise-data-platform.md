## What’s new and why it matters
Why One-Size-Fits-All Data Platforms Often Fail Many enterprise data platforms do not fail because the technology is poor. They fail because they are designed around the needs of only one group of users. For example, a platform may work perfectly for data engineers but be too complicated for a CFO who simply needs accurate numbers on a dashboard. Similarly, analysts may have access to attractive reports but struggle when the underlying data pipelines are slow, unreliable, or poorly managed.
