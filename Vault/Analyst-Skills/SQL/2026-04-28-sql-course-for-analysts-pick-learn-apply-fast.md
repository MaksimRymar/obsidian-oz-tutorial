## What’s new and why it matters
Hiring managers don’t care that you “know SQL” — they care that you can answer business questions quickly and correctly. If you’re searching for a sql course for analysts , you’re probably trying to bridge that exact gap: turning tables into decisions without getting lost in database theory. What “SQL for analysts” actually means (and what it doesn’t) Analyst SQL is not backend-engineering SQL. You don’t need to design a perfect schema or tune indexes on day one.
