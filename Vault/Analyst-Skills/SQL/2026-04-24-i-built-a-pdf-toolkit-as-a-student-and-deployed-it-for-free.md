## What’s new and why it matters
Flask, PyMuPDF, Groq, and a lot of jugaad engineering Every student has been there. It's 11 PM. You need to compress a PDF before submitting it, convert a JPEG to PDF for a form, or quickly summarize a 40-page document before an exam. You open some sketchy website, it watermarks your file, asks you to pay, and uploads your documents to who-knows-where. I got tired of it. So I built my own. DocFlask is an all-in-one document toolkit built with Flask. It handles PDF merging, splitting, conversion, compression, image conversion, and even AI-powered summarization and quiz generation — all for free…
