## What’s new and why it matters
You'll often find that the data you need is spread across multiple tables or requires complex calculations that simple GROUP BY queries can't handle. Two of the most powerful tools in your SQL toolbox for solving these challenges are JOINS and Window Functions . While Joins allow us to stitch together data from multiple tables, Window Functions allow us to perform complex calculations across rows without losing granularity of our data.
