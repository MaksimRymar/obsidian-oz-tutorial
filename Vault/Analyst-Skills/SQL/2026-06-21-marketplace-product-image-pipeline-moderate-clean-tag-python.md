## What’s new and why it matters
A marketplace lives or dies on its catalog, and the catalog is built from images other people upload. Every seller photo needs three things before it belongs on your storefront: a safety check so prohibited products never go live, a consistent look so the catalog does not resemble a flea market, and tags so the item is findable. Doing that by hand does not scale. Three APIs, chained into one Python pipeline, do it automatically for every upload.
