## What’s new and why it matters
The problem nobody is solving AI agents can write code, browse the web, send emails. But ask a simple question: "How does this agent prove its identity to another service?" — and you'll hit a wall. Modern identity systems (OAuth, SSO, API keys) are built for humans who click buttons in a browser. An autonomous agent needs something different: Portable credentials that can be carried across platforms. A way to prove identity to other agents and services without human intervention.
