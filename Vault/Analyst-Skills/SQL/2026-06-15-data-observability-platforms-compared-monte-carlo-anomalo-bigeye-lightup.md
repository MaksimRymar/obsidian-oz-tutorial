## What’s new and why it matters
Data observability platforms are the layer between "we test the data" and "we trust the data," and the four names every buyer hears in 2026 — Monte Carlo, Anomalo, Bigeye, and Lightup — represent four very different bets on what that layer should look like. The category started as a marketing rebrand of "data quality monitoring" in 2020 and has since hardened into a real product surface: continuous freshness, volume, schema, distribution, and lineage signals piped into the same incident channels as the rest of your on-call rotation. The buying decision is no longer "should we have a platform"…
