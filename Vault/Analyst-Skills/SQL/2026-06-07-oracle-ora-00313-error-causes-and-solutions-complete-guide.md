## What’s new and why it matters
ORA-00313: Open Failed for Members of Log Group of Thread ORA-00313 is a critical Oracle database error that occurs when Oracle cannot open one or more members of a Redo Log Group during database startup or a log switch operation. This typically means the physical Redo Log file is missing, corrupted, or inaccessible due to permission or mount issues.
