## What’s new and why it matters
ORA-00289: Archive Log Destination – Causes, Fixes & Prevention ORA-00289 is an informational/warning message from Oracle indicating a suggested or problematic archive log destination. It typically appears alongside other archiver-related errors such as ORA-00255 or ORA-00257, signaling that the Archiver process (ARCn) cannot write archive logs to the configured destination.
