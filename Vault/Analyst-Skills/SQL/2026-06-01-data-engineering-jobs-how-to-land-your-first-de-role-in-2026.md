## What’s new and why it matters
data engineering jobs are still the highest-paying entry into the modern data stack, but the 2026 market looks nothing like the gold-rush hiring of 2021–2023. Recruiters now field 400+ applicants per posting, ATS systems pre-filter ~70% before a human ever sees the resume, and the interview loop runs 5 rounds instead of 3. The candidates who land offers in 90–180 days are not the ones with the most degrees — they are the ones who treat the search like a system: a sized funnel, a calibrated resume, a weekly outreach cadence, and an interview loop they've rehearsed end-to-end.
