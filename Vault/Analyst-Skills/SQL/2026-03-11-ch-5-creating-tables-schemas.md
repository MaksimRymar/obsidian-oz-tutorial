## What’s new and why it matters
We are using DBeaver as our GUI to interact with our database. We have already created the PostgreSQL dtabase service using Aiven . The first step is powering on the database service in Aiven. Just head to Aiven, then services, and on the database you created, click on the three dots in the action tab and select 'power on service'. Once the connection is running, head over to DBeaver. Right-click on your database, select SQL Editor, then open a New SQL script.
