## What’s new and why it matters
reverse etl is the discipline that closes the loop a data team starts the first time it lands raw events in a warehouse and then realises the warehouse, however beautiful, is invisible to the GTM team. Forward ETL moved source data into the warehouse so analysts could ask questions; reverse ETL ships the answers back out into the operational tools — Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Intercom, Slack, Facebook Ads, Iterable — where the people and systems that act on customers actually live.
