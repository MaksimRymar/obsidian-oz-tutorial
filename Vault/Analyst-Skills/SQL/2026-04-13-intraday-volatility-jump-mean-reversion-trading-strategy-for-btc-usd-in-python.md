## What’s new and why it matters
Volatility jumps are one of the most exploitable microstructure phenomena in cryptocurrency markets. When Bitcoin experiences a sudden, outsized price move relative to its recent realized volatility, it frequently reverts — not because of any fundamental force, but because liquidity providers reprice, over-leveraged positions unwind, and the market stabilizes. The Intraday Volatility Jump Mean-Reversion (JMR) strategy systematically detects these statistical outliers and fades them, positioning against the jump and waiting for the reversion.
