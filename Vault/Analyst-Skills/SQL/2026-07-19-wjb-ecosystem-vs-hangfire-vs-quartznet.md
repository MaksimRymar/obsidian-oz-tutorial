## What’s new and why it matters
Why I Would Choose WJb The .NET ecosystem already has two well-known background processing solutions: Hangfire and Quartz.NET . Both are mature, battle-tested, and widely adopted. However, the WJb Ecosystem takes a fundamentally different approach. Instead of focusing solely on scheduling jobs, WJb focuses on building a lightweight, high-performance, and extensible execution platform with a clean separation between execution, storage, hosting, and monitoring.
