## What’s new and why it matters
ORA-00447: Fatal Error in Background Process — Causes, Fixes & Prevention ORA-00447 is one of Oracle's most severe errors, signaling that a critical failure has occurred within a background process such as PMON, SMON, LGWR, or DBWR. Unlike typical SQL errors, this error directly threatens instance stability and almost always results in an instance crash.
