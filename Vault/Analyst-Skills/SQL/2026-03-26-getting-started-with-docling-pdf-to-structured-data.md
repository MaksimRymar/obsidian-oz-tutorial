## What’s new and why it matters
Docling is an open-source document conversion tool from IBM Research . It takes PDFs and converts them into clean, structured output like Markdown, HTML, JSON, or plain text. It handles layout analysis, table extraction, image embedding, OCR, and even a vision-based pipeline for complex documents. This guide walks through installation, the core conversion options, and the advanced flags worth knowing.
