ORA-00922: Missing or Invalid Option — Causes and Fixes ORA-00922 is a syntax error thrown by the Oracle database when a DDL or SQL statement contains an unrecognized or incorrectly placed option or keyword. It most commonly appears with CREATE , ALTER , or DROP statements when the parser cannot interpret a clause or option.
